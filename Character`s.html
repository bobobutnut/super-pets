Keith & Mark

They are former school pets who became ice/water and fire elemental mutant henchmen after receiving powers from Lulu by using her orange kryptonite crystal and after she betrayed them, they were later adopted by Aquaman and Cyborg.

Merton the Turtle

Merton the Turtle / Terrific Whatzit appears in DC League of Super-Pets, voiced by Natasha Lyonne. This version is a non-anthropomorphic female turtle who acquires super-speed after being exposed to orange Kryptonite and goes on to become the Flash's pet and a founding member of the titular League of Super-Pets.

Ace

Ace is a fairly cynical dog, reckless, outspoken, irritable and skeptical at first but beneath his toughest exterior he is good-hearted and very emotional although he doesn't always show it.
